The 20-20-20 Rule

Your simple daily habit to fight digital eye strain and keep your vision sharp.

⏱️

Every 20 Minutes

Take a break from your screen every 20 minutes of continuous use.

👀

Look 20 Feet Away

Focus your eyes on something at least 20 feet (6 meters) away.

🕐

For 20 Seconds

Hold your gaze for at least 20 seconds to fully relax the eye muscles.
